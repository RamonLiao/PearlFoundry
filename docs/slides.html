01 · The Problem

Retail is locked out of a trillion-dollar market

  • Bank notes demand $50k–$250k minimums, lock-ups and heavy KYC.
  • First-gen DeFi option vaults — Ribbon, Cega — collapsed on oracle manipulation and no real volatility surface.
  • Neither side gives buyers an immutable, auditable record of the terms they bought.
02 · The Solution

PearlFoundry fixes all three on Sui

  • Atomic PTB — bundle multiple DeepBook Predict legs in a single transaction. No leg-risk, no slippage.
  • On-chain SVI pricing — every note priced against DeepBook's live volatility surface, not a guessed number.
  • Soulbound note object — a tamper-proof prospectus that can't be edited after the fact.

03 · Demo — Mint

One-click mint

  • Pricing engine reads the live SVI forward, builds a multi-leg ladder of adjacent strikes hugging it.
  • Two PTBs: create the shared Predict manager, then a hot-potato builder — mint_begin → add_expiry × N → mint_finalize, all atomic.
  • Out comes a soulbound note — transfer-locked, claimable only by me.
03 · Demo — Claim

Settle & claim

  • A notify-only watcher tails chain events and flags matured notes.
  • A keeper structurally cannot claim for you — the note is an owned soulbound object, so only the owner signs.
  • claim_begin → settle each leg → claim_finalize: settle, withdraw payout, take the 10% performance fee, burn the note — atomically.
03 · Demo — Ledger

The Nacre Ledger

  • Connect, and your own row lights up, tagged ‘YOU’.
  • The SocialFi hook: issuers build a public, verifiable track record of realised returns.
04 · Roadmap

Where it goes next
